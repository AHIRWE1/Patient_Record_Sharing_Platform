Our Mission

To make healthcare systems empathetic, dignified, and humane again - using technology to enhance the management, access, and sharing of patient information among healthcare professionals in Rwanda and across Africa.

What is this platform?

This platform enables secure sharing of patient records between hospitals, improving continuity of care and reducing repeated medical tests. Healthcare facilities in Rwanda like many across Africa - often operate on isolated patient record systems, meaning that when a patient transfers or seeks care elsewhere, their history is incomplete or unavailable. MediShare Rwanda solves this by providing a centralised, secure web-based system where authorised medical workers can retrieve and exchange patient records across institutions.

The Problem
  • Patients lose records when transferring between hospitals
  • Doctors repeat tests because previous results are unavailable
  • Isolated systems with no interoperability between facilities
  • Treatment delays due to incomplete patient histories
  • Higher healthcare costs from redundant procedures
Our Solution
  • Centralised, secure digital patient record storage
  • Approved inter-hospital record sharing (view-only)
  • Role-based access - right information to the right people
  • Full audit trail for accountability and compliance
  • Scalable from Rwanda to the rest of Africa

Context

Rwanda is the first implementation site due to its strong investment in digital health infrastructure and national health information systems. This prototype is designed to be scalable across Africa to address similar challenges in other countries. Built as part of an academic project at African Leadership University.
