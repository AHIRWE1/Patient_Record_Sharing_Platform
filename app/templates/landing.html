Everything your hospital needs

Designed for Rwanda's healthcare reality - simple, secure, and scalable across Africa.

Secure Authentication

Role-based login (Admin, Staff, Doctor) with bcrypt password hashing ensures only authorised staff access records.

Patient Registration

Register patients with name, date of birth, gender, and national ID. Each patient gets a unique identifier automatically.

Medical Records

Doctors add diagnoses, test results, treatments, and clinical notes. All records are preserved — nothing is ever overwritten.

Inter-Hospital Sharing

Send a secure share request to another hospital. They approve it and gain view-only access to the patient's history.

Full Audit Trail

Every login, record access, update, and sharing action is timestamped and logged for full accountability.

Mobile Friendly

Fully responsive - works on desktop, tablet, and mobile phones so staff can access records from any device.

How it works

Get your hospital set up in three simple steps.

1
Register your hospital

Create a hospital account with one admin. The admin then adds staff and doctors with role-specific access.

2
Register patients & update records

Staff register patients at admission. Doctors add diagnoses, tests, and treatment plans - building a complete, timestamped history.

3
Share records during transfer

When a patient moves to another hospital, send a secure sharing request. The receiving hospital approves and instantly sees the full history - no paperwork, no delays.
