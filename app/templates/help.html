Help & User Guide

Quick start: If this is your first time, begin by registering your hospital, then add your staff and doctors, and finally start registering patients. This guide explains each step in detail below.
Hospital Admin

Manages the hospital account and users

  • Register the hospital
  • Add staff & doctors
  • Approve share requests
  • View audit logs
Hospital Staff

Handles patient registration and sharing

  • Register new patients
  • Search patient records
  • Send share requests
  • Approve incoming shares
Doctor

Updates clinical records for patients

  • View patient history
  • Add diagnosis & tests
  • Add treatment plans
  • Add clinical notes

Step-by-Step Guides

How to Register a Patient
  1. Log in as Staff or Admin.
  2. In the sidebar, click Patients.
  3. Click the New Patient button (top right).
  4. Fill in:
    • Full name
    • Date of birth
    • Gender
    • National ID (must be unique)
  5. Click Register Patient. The patient now appears in the list.
How to Update Medical Records
  1. Log in as a Doctor.
  2. In the sidebar, click Records.
  3. Find the patient in the table and click Add.
  4. Fill in the record form:
    • Diagnosis
    • Test results
    • Treatment plan
    • Clinical notes (optional)
  5. Click Save Record. Previous records are preserved — nothing is overwritten.
How to Share Records with Another Hospital
  1. Log in as Staff or Admin.
  2. In the sidebar, click Share Records.
  3. Select the patient to share from the dropdown.
  4. Select the target hospital to send the request to.
  5. Click Send Request.
  6. The other hospital sees the request in their Incoming Requests panel and clicks Approve. After approval they can view the patient's records.
How to Add Staff or Doctors (Admin only)
  1. Log in as Admin.
  2. In the sidebar, click Manage Users.
  3. Click Add User.
  4. Enter:
    • Username (must be unique across all hospitals)
    • Password
    • Role: Staff or Doctor
  5. Click Add User. The new user can now log in.
